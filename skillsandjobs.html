About us

Team Thirteen is seeking an experienced Graphic Designer to design all the graphical requirements for ToDoTopia.

ToDoTopia's goal is to create a web-based tool that integrates with workplace productivity, work-in-progress, and time-tracking software to gamify individual employee and team responsibilities. In the end, the application will be a web-based computer games platform that allows users to earn experience points (xp) for completed tasks. Employees and Teams are encouraged by accumulating experience points (Xp) that can be used to develop things in a computer game, such as characters by gamifying daily job duties.

Job Description

We're looking to add a fresh graphic designer to our team. You'll be designing a wide range of items for both digital and traditional media. You must be a self-starter, capable of providing fantastic creative ideas, and have exceptional attention to detail to be successful in this position.
